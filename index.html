How CryptoCharge Works

From stablecoins to Indian recharges.

In Phase 1, CryptoCharge connects your on-chain stablecoin payment to traditional recharge APIs in the background. You deposit once, then do multiple instant recharges from your in-app wallet.

Step 1
Deposit stablecoins into your wallet

You receive a deposit address for USDT/USDC on supported chains. When your funds arrive, your CryptoCharge wallet balance is updated.

Step 2
Recharge instantly from balance

Enter your recharge details and pay directly from your wallet balance. No need to wait for a fresh on-chain confirmation each time.

Step 3
Withdraw any unused funds

If you have remaining USDT/USDC, you can request a withdrawal back to your own wallet. CryptoCharge sends it on-chain.
